Overview

Many assistance programs in the United States provide tablets to qualifying individuals and households. These initiatives are often linked to EBT, Medicaid, Lifeline, and ACP (Affordable Connectivity Program) benefits.

The following resources organize detailed guides, program overviews, and user experiences to help you understand how to apply, what benefits are available, and who qualifies.

Quick FAQ

Who qualifies for a free government tablet?
Eligibility usually depends on income level, participation in programs like EBT or Medicaid, or household enrollment in ACP or Lifeline.

Do tablets always come with internet access?
Not always, some programs include service plans, while others provide devices only. Check the program details before applying.

Is it safe to apply through online forms?
Yes, but only use trusted program sites and official links. Avoid sharing personal information on unverified platforms.
